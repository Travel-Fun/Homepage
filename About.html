Travel Fun

About Us

Travel Fun is a world tour website that allows users to share their precious memories and moments while traveling with people from all around the world. On the website, visitors can drag the map and click on different areas to see photos or posts of that region from other people.

Traveling with Travel Fun

As a platform that aims for sharing, Travel Fun has two modes, the Viewer mode, and the User mode

Viewer mode

In the Viewer mode, people can only view posts, photos, videos, audios, and comments uploaded by other users. These posts shared by the users are the memories of the author after visiting the corresponding areas or the plan they set for traveling.

User mode

On the other hand, the User mode allows the users to log in to the website. All functions are available in User mode, users are able to upload all kinds of media after signing in to the website. Moreover, they can comment or discuss on other posts.

What we are trying to work on . . . .

Users can mark their destination or set a memo for their travel plan on the map. With the mark, the specific map area will be colored with a color. Lastly, everytime the user visits a new country, the corresponding location of the country on the map will light up. The more locations users have traveled and posted, the higher badge level will get. Other users will see your badge on your portfolio.
